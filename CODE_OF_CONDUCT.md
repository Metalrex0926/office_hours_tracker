# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus feedback on code and behavior, not individuals.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Repeated disruptive behavior in issues/PRs.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation for violations.
